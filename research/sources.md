# High-Signal Experts: AI-Powered SEO for B2B SaaS

### Selection Criteria

I specifically filtered for operators, agency founders, and in-house growth leads who are actively testing AI models against Google's Helpful Content updates, rather than theoretical "hustle culture" influencers.

1. **Kevin Indig** (Former Director of SEO at Shopify)

   * **Focus:** Enterprise B2B SEO, AI search generative experience (SGE), and growth models.

   * **Why:** Practitioner with a track record of scaling massive B2B/B2C tech companies.

2. **Aleyda Solis** (International SEO Consultant & Founder of Orainti)

   * **Focus:** Technical SEO, automating SEO workflows with AI prompts.

   * **Why:** Consistently publishes hands-on, highly technical tests of AI tools against search algorithms.

3. **Bernard Huang** (Co-founder of Clearscope)

   * **Focus:** Search intent, AI-driven content optimization, and semantic SEO.

   * **Why:** Builds the actual AI tools B2B companies use to rank; deep understanding of natural language processing.

4. **Gael Breton** (Co-founder of Authority Hacker)

   * **Focus:** Scalable AI content systems, prompt engineering for editorial quality.

   * **Why:** Runs massive portfolio sites and openly tests AI content pipelines and their traffic retention.

5. **Eli Schwartz** (Author of Product-Led SEO)

   * **Focus:** B2B SEO strategy, building programmable content.

   * **Why:** Focuses on using AI for programmatic SEO (building tools/databases) rather than just generating blog posts.

6. **Lily Ray** (Vice President, SEO Strategy at Amsive Digital)

   * **Focus:** E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) in the age of AI.

   * **Why:** The leading voice on how Google penalizes lazy AI content and how B2B brands can inject human expertise.

7. **Ross Hudgens** (Founder of Siege Media)

   * **Focus:** B2B content marketing, AI in content velocity.

   * **Why:** Runs a premier B2B content agency; highly critical and realistic about what AI can and cannot do for brand growth.

8. **Jess Scholz** (Former CMO at Ringier)

   * **Focus:** SEO strategy pivoting for AI Search (Perplexity, ChatGPT).

9. **Mark Williams-Cook** (Founder of AlsoAsked)

   * **Focus:** Intent research, scaling AI content ethically.

   * **Why:** Combines search intent data with AI automation to capture long-tail B2B queries.

10. **Tommy Griffith** (Founder of ClickMinded, ex-Airbnb SEO)

    * **Focus:** SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) for SEO.

    * **Why:** Focuses on the operational side—how to actually build the team and workflows to execute AI SEO pipelines.
