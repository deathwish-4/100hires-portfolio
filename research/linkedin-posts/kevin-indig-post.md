Your business website costs $5K and 6 weeks. Mine cost $0 and took an afternoon.  
  
The old way:  
  
- $5,000 to a developer  
- 6 weeks of "almost done" emails  
- $30-99/month hosting  
- $30-60/year per plugin (and you need like 5-10 of them)  
- Monthly WordPress maintenance anxiety  
- Want a change? Pay the dev again. If they answer.  
  
What I did instead: talked to Claude Code.  
  
Described what I wanted. It generated 11 different homepage concepts. I picked one, told it what I liked, and it built a full design system from that.  
  
The result?  
  
Every page after that built in MINUTES. Not days. Minutes. Because Claude already knew the fonts, colors, spacing, components.  
  
About page? One prompt.  
  
And here's what you get for $0/month on Cloudflare:  
  
- Forms that work  
- SEO baked in  
- Blog ready to go  
- Enterprise CDN  
- 5,000 free image optimizations/month  
- Handles 70K-100K visits before you pay a cent  
  
Zero WordPress plugins. Zero monthly hosting bills. Zero emailing your developer on a Sunday hoping they respond by Wednesday.  
  
We built a 15-lesson course in our AI Accelerator teaching non-technical people to do exactly this.  
  
I genuinely don't know what the argument for paying $5K is at this point.
